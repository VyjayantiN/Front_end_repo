Ponds in Each Region

  • Bengaluru: Bengaluru's fertile soil benefits from a well-developed water resource management system, including lakes, reservoirs, and underground water sources.
  • Mysuru: Mysuru's agricultural fertility is supported by the Cauvery River and its tributaries, ensuring reliable water supply for irrigation.
  • Hubli: Hubli's fertility is attributed to the presence of the Krishna River, which facilitates irrigation and sustains agricultural productivity.
  • Mangaluru: Mangaluru's coastal location benefits from the proximity to the Arabian Sea, ensuring adequate water resources for agriculture and horticulture.
  • Belagavi: Belagavi's fertile soil is nourished by rivers like Krishna and Ghataprabha, providing water for irrigation and promoting agricultural growth.
