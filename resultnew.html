How to Determine Age of tree?

To determine the age of a tree, various methods can be employed, depending on the species and available information. Here are a few common techniques:.

  • Counting tree rings (Dendrochronology)
  • Increment borer
  • Growth rate estimation.
How to determine species?

Growth rate estimation: By measuring the circumference or diameter of the tree trunk and comparing it to the growth rate of similar trees, an approximate age can be estimated. However, this method is less accurate than counting tree rings.
